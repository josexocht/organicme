Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions:

  • Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Cut the butternut squash in half and remove the seeds. Place the squash cut side down on a baking sheet and roast for 30 to 40 minutes, or until tender. Let cool.
  • In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened.
  • Add the broth and bring to a boil. Add the roasted butternut squash and puree the mixture until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve and enjoy!

    • Organic Pizza

    Ingredients:

  • 1 lb organic pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup organic tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated organic mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated organic parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped organic basil
  • 1/4 cup sliced organic mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced organic bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced organic onions
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions:

  • Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a little bit of space around the edges.
  • Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, basil, mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions over the sauce.
  • Drizzle the olive oil over the toppings and season with salt and pepper.
  • Carefully transfer the pizza to the oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  • Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.li>
  • Enjoy your organic pizza!

    • Organic Burger

    Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh herbs
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion or shallot
  • 1-2 cloves minced garlic
  • 500gr of organic grass-fed beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Red onion slices
  • Tomato slices

    • Instructions:

  • Add flavorings to the organic ground beef.
  • Blend just enough to distribute, then gently reshape the beef burger patties.
  • Cook the organic grass fed burgers over hot coals about 3-4 minutes for medium-rare, or more to desired doneness.
  • Set on buns and serve with fixings.
